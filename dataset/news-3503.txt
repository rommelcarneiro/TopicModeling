


In this episode of Modern Love: The Podcast, the actress Maria Bello (“Coyote Ugly,” “A History of Violence,” “ER”) reads her own Modern Love essay, “Coming Out as a Modern Family.”

You can also hear the episode on iTunes and Stitcher.

After, hear Ms. Bello talk about how she’s doing now. “Whomever I love,” she wrote, “however I love them, whether they sleep in my bed or not, or whether I do homework with them or share a child with them, ‘love is love.’ And I love our modern family.”
