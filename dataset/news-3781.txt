


Laura Caitlin Flusty and Jacob Reed Smilovitz were married May 28 at the Knollwood Country Club in West Bloomfield, Mich. Rabbi Joshua Bennett officiated.

The couple met at the University of Michigan, from which they graduated with distinction.

The bride, 25, will continue to use her name professionally. She works in consumer marketing for American Express in New York, overseeing the creation and distribution of marketing materials to new cardmembers. She is the daughter of Wendy G. Flusty and Richard A. Flusty of Farmington Hills, Mich.

The groom, 26, is a senior manager in the strategy and development group of The New York Times, where he helps manage mergers and acquisitions and other corporate strategies. He is a son of Donna Rockwell Smilovitz and Bernie Smilovitz of Franklin, Mich.
