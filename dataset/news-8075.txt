


Anna Marin Steffens, the daughter of Zita Ingham and Ronald E. Steffens of Bandon, Ore., is to be married April 24 to Katharine Elizabeth Uva, a daughter of Ken Uva of Forest Hills, Queens, and the late Shelley Uva. Shannon Sun-Higginson, a Universal Life minister and a friend of the couple, is to officiate at the Gil Hodges Community Garden in Brooklyn.

Ms. Steffens (left), 27, is the community school coordinator at East Brooklyn Community High School, a public transfer high school in Brooklyn. She graduated from Barnard College and received a master's degree in social work from Hunter College.

Her father is an associate professor of communications at Green Mountain College in Poultney, Vt. He also works as a seasonal fire manager at Grand Teton National Park in Wyoming and is chairman of the editorial board of Wildfire magazine. Her mother retired as an English professor at Southwestern Oregon Community College in Coos Bay, Ore.

Ms. Uva, 28, is studying for a Ph.D. in American history at the City University of New York Graduate Center. She also works as an Andrew W. Mellon Foundation predoctoral history education fellow at the Museum of the City of New York in Manhattan. She graduated from Boston University.

Her father retired as a vice president and corporate counsel for CT Corporation, a legal services company in Manhattan.

The couple met in 2008 on OkCupid.
