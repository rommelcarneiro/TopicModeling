


Peter Edward Kukielski and Jonathan Drew Hodges were married April 22 at City Hall in Portland, Me., where Teresa J. Bunn, a records clerk, officiated.

Mr. Kukielski (left), 50, is a horticulturalist and garden designer in Portland. He is also the author of “Roses Without Chemicals.” He served as the curator of the Peggy Rockefeller Rose Garden at the New York Botanical Garden in the Bronx from 2006 to 2013. He graduated from Young Harris College in Georgia.

He is a son of the late Elizabeth J. Kukielski and the late Robert A. Kukielski, who lived in Tucker, Ga.

Mr. Hodges, 53, is known as Drew. He is the founder of SpotCo Advertising in Manhattan. He graduated from the School of Visual Arts in Manhattan.

He is a son of Kathleen E. Hodges and A. Donald Hodges of New Bern, N.C.

The couple met in February 2008 at an event held at the New York Botanical Garden.
