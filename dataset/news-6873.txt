


In this week’s episode of Modern Love: The Podcast, Cheryl Strayed, the author of “Wild” and a host of the podcast “Dear Sugar,” reads Mary Elizabeth Williams’s 2014 essay “A Second Embrace, With Hearts and Eyes Open,” about a divorced couple who reunite to experience life and love through a new perspective.

You can also hear the episode on iTunes and Stitcher.

Afterward, listen to a conversation between Ms. Williams, a journalist and author of the new memoir “A Series of Catastrophes and Miracles,” and the Modern Love editor, Dan Jones.
